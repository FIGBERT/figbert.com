It took me two decades to learn that coconut water does not taste like
sweat. Rather, some bottles on the grocery store shelf are full of sweat
while others are not. I tried them all so now I can tell you which is
which.

<!-- more -->

1. [Once Upon a Coconut](https://onceuponacoconut.com/)
2. [Coaqua](https://www.drinkcoaqua.com/)
3. [Harmless Harvest](https://harmlessharvest.com/)

These are the kings of coconut water. Somehow, they manage to taste
_toasted_. I don't understand how you do that. Delicious.

Notes and caveats: Do not try the flavored versions. Good coconut
water is like good sushi—simple is better. Also Coaqua's brand copy is
extremely cringe. And why is Harmless Harvest pink?

4. [C2O](https://drinkc2o.com/)

I really like the corrugated metal look but the flavor is, in my view,
unremarkable. Nageena's favorite.

5. Taste Nirvana, Parrot, etc.

I would rather have normal water than these, unless I'm feeling
desperate or it's been a while.

6. Vita Coco

Do not buy under any circumstances. Despicable, vile drink.
